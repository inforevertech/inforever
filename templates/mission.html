What is Inforever? Why is it special?

Inforever is a post-sharing platform utilizing the Bitcoin blockchain as the protocol for data storage. This approach presents some unique advantages, such as censorship resistance, imperishability of records, and unrestricted public access to data for everyone who has access to the blockchain.

Why do we exploit only Bitcoin blockchain?

Bitcoin blockchain is the time-tested decentralized ledger relying on its community without any explicit leaders. Thus, we believe that utilizing the Bitcoin protocol is the most trustworthy way to share value units (satoshis) and suitable information.

Why do we keep all code open source?

While regular web users are having a hard time protecting their privacy online, we are convinced that everyone has the right to know how the software they are using was built. Thus, to be completely honest with our users, we keep our codebase accessible for everyone to explore, share and expand.

Why is testnet network kept available?

To allow users to experiment with the platform first without spending valuable satoshis, we decided to keep the Bitcoin test network accessible for all users of Inforever. Thus, everyone can get a taste of posting and exploring relevant data on the blockchain.
